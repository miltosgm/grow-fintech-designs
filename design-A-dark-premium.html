FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with early-stage fintechs or only growth-stage?

Both. We work with seed-stage fintechs building their first marketing engine and Series B+ companies scaling into new markets. The engagement structure differs, but our fintech focus stays the same.

How do you handle financial advertising compliance?

Every campaign asset is reviewed against applicable financial advertising regulations (FCA, SEC, CFPB, MAS depending on your market). We've never had a client campaign flagged for compliance violations.

What's your typical engagement model?

We work on monthly retainers for ongoing growth partnerships and project-based engagements for specific initiatives. Minimum engagement is 3 months.

Do you work with B2B fintechs, B2C, or both?

Both. We have dedicated approaches for B2B fintech (enterprise sales cycles, ABM, LinkedIn) and B2C fintech (performance marketing, lifecycle, retention). The strategies are very different — we don't mix them up.

How quickly can you get started?

We can typically onboard within 5–7 business days. Our Strategy Sprint in week one means you're seeing outputs before most agencies have finished their "kickoff deck."
